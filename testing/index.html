Welcome to Vorlage.

The Vorlage team at Ellem really apprecieate your adoption of the Vorlage Webengine. We strive to make the Vorlage the best webengine out there, but we cannot do it without you!

Please note that the nature of Vorlage is not very friendly to those newer to software development, this is only because Vorlage is in the earlier stages of development and some things still need to be figured out until we start writting fancy manuals and help guides outside the specification.
